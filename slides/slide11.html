Generating SMILES: Cubane (1/4)

The Challenge: A 3D Cage

Cubane (C₈H₈) is a synthetic hydrocarbon with its eight carbon atoms arranged at the corners of a cube. This unique, highly-strained structure presents a fun challenge for SMILES notation.

Our goal is to convert this complex 3D shape into a simple, linear text string that accurately represents its atomic connections.

Narration: Let's tackle a more complex molecule: Cubane. As you can see, it's a three-dimensional cage, with its eight carbon atoms arranged at the corners of a cube.
