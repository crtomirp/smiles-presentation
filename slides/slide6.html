Common Mistakes to Avoid

Aromatic vs. Aliphatic

Using uppercase 'C' for benzene instead of lowercase 'c'.

C1CCCCC1 vs c1ccccc1

Ring Closure Errors

Forgetting to close a ring or using mismatched numbers.

c1cccc vs c1ccccc1

Misplaced Branches

Placing parentheses incorrectly, breaking the main chain.

C(C)CC vs CC(C)C

Stereochemistry Omission

Ignoring chiral centers when the information is critical.

C[C]H(O)C vs C[C@H](O)C
